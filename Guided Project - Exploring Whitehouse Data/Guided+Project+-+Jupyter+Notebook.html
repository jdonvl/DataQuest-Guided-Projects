Based on what we can see above, there may be a correlation between the length of the Position Titles and salary.

In [24]:
positionlength = whitehouse["Position Title"].apply(len)
whitehouse["Position Length"] = positionlength

plt.scatter(whitehouse["Position Length"], whitehouse["Salary"])
plt.xlabel('Position Title Length')
plt.ylabel("Salary Per Annum")

The scatter shows a high concentration of Salaries < 100K having Position Titles with less than ~60 characters. Salaries above 100K appear to have Position Titles with more than ~60 characters in general.
